How to find your ICC friends

A facebook- and a whatsapp group have been created for all of you, staff, participants and volunteers, where you can discuss everything, chat about everything, and try to find each other physically.

  • The whatsapp group can be reached with the following link: https://is.gd/iccmeetingroomwhatsapp.
    When clicking on it, you will be redirected to whatsapp, where you can join the ICC2017 meeting room.
  • The facebook link to the group is: https://is.gd/iccmeetingroomfb.
    It might be that you have to put this link into the address bar of your browser, and then solve a captcha. Anyway, you can also find the group by searching (icc 2017 meeting room)
